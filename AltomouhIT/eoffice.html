Solution Overview

A paperless office with functionality viz records, document & drawing management, document registration, electronic folders, security controlled by Active Directory, automatic updating of user profile information from the active directory, user permissions, audit trails, version control, web browser access, maps & plans scanning, annotation & editing and many more. An end to end office automation solution through which all the incoming and outgoing correspondences (emails, faxes, documents, maps, plans and softcopy files) maintained in a single repository.

Faster and better communication technologies have brought many changes in the way we conduct business. It is expected that you have access to key information from wherever you are, that’s why results-oriented organizations are putting enterprise automation solutions in place that brings together people, processes and documents to improve efficiency, reduce costs and meet compliance requirements and this is where AL TOMOUH comes into picture to help you streamline the services towards your valued customers, increase profits and simplify compliances by eliminating paper from your business processes.

Doesn’t matter what business you do, documents such as file noting, purchase orders, claims forms, invoices, and applications are crucial. They can also be overwhelming for workers and business processes. Often the problem is not the content of the documents. It’s the paper shuffle that bogs an organization down.

Let’s consider for a minute the typical day in an organization.

  • People perform tasks, route information and make decisions as they complete business activities. In most organizations, this is a paper-centric process.
  • Documents are printed out, filled in or annotated, placed in folders, then manually routed and re-routed around the office as tasks are acted upon and approved.
  • Then as documents are shared with customers and suppliers they are emailed or manually faxed or sent by post.
  • Finally, they are copied and stored in filling cabinets and storage rooms around the office or at offsite storage facilities for later retrieval or destruction.t
  • Drawbacks of such paper-based processes are too many.

In this process every step invokes is inefficient, time consuming, error prone and filled with potential security holes.

Documents may be lost, misrouted, and the important decisions get stuck in a bottleneck, creating a tensed environment between the customers/citizen, suppliers and employees causing serious problems, including high costs and service delays as well as compliance and legal exposure.
