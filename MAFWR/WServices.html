BP-TS-13 - Request for General Services outside the Segment Limit

Process Name Request for General Services outside the Segment Limit
Process ID BP-TS- 13
Application processing
  1. The ministry receives the building permit application form with all required documents attached.
  2. Schedule an appointment of the field visit to carry out technical inspection of the site.
  3. Conduct the site inspection and prepare the technical report for the site.
  4. Approve the permit and detailed maps by the municipal engineer.
  5. The required fees are paid by the applicant and the application is transferred to the municipality director for approval.
  6. Issues the building permit to the consultant and a copy of permit and maps is kept in the municipality.
  7. The applicant is notified with the process steps, additional required documents or clarifications needed with SMS and Email services.
Required Documents
  1. Ownership document ( Mulkiya )
  2. Survey Sketch (Krooki)
  3. Drawing of Structural and Architectural
  4. Structural Design Calculations
  5. Govt.Bodies approval
  6. Supervising Consultant Report
Process Duration 3 to 10 working days
